A Titanic Introduction To R
This workshop is introduces new useRs to R. Introduces concepts such as primary data types, vectors, data.frames, indexing, filtering and performing simple statistical analyses such as the Student's T-test and and the Chi-Squared test.
Women and Children First! A Crash Course in Logistic Regression
This workshop is the next-step after the Titanic Introduction. Reviews the data management skills previously introduced and builds upon them with a real-world analytical project. Participants learn how to build a linear model to understand factors affecting passenger survival, such as gender and age. At the end of the workshop, participants use this model to predict survival against a provided test data set.
