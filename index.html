Titanic bow

In the span of only a few years, R has become the lingua franca of statistics. The Titanic Introduction To R Workshops are a series of three hour cruises designed to introduce new users to R. Participants are not expected to have prior programming experience, but they are expected to have a basic understanding of the statistical concepts discussed during the workshop they are taking.

To learn more about specific workshops, go to the Workshops page.
